May is traditionally a month of devotion to Our Blessed Lady.  Throughout the month, at the end of every Mass, we shall recite the following prayer, which dates from the third century and is the oldest known prayer to Our Lady:-

 

We fly to thy Patronage, O holy Mother of God.  Despise not our prayers in our necessities but deliver us from all dangers, O glorious and blessed Virgin.

 

 

Remember, O most loving Virgin Mary,

that it is a thing unheard of

that anyone ever had recourse to thy protection,

implored thy help, or sought thy intercession,

and was left forsaken.

Filled, therefore, with confidence in thy goodness,

I fly to thee, O Mother, Virgin of virgins;

to thee I come, before thee I stand, a sorrowful sinner.

Despise not my poor words, O Mother of the Word of God,

but graciously hear and grant my prayer.

Amen.
