May is traditionally a month of devotion to Our Blessed Lady.  Throughout the month, at the end of every Mass, we shall recite the following prayer, which dates from the third century and is the oldest known prayer to Our Lady:-

 

We fly to thy Patronage, O holy Mother of God.  Despise not our prayers in our necessities but deliver us from all dangers, O glorious and blessed Virgin.

 

 

When the most high, descending, confounded the tongues, he divided the nations; when he distributed the tongues of fire, he called all men into unity: therefore, with one voice, we glorify the most holy Spirit.

Blessed art thou, O Christ our God, who didst give wisdom to the Fishers of men, by sending upon them the Holy Spirit, and by them dost net all the world: glory be to thee.

Be thou exalted, O Lord in thy Power: we will praise and magnify thy mighty acts: O blessed Paraclete, save us, who sing to thee: Alleluia.

Byzantine Rite
