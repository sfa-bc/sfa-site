The notice about the 150th anniversary of the building of the present church of St Francis in 2020 elicited very little response, certainly nothing that would require any planning at this stage.  We will keep this under review, and details of the proposed re-decoration of the church will be made available in due course, but it seems that we shall be keeping any celebrations in 2020 relatively low key.
