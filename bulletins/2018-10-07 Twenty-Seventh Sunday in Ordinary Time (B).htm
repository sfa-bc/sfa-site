The Society for the Protection of Unborn Children has produced postcards so that people can send a message to their local MP urging him or her to vote against a proposed bill that is coming up in the House of Commons on 23rd October.  The bill seeks to decriminalise abortion, thus removing any effective legal protection for unborn babies and their mothers in England, Wales and northern Ireland.

The postcards are available at the back of church.

To know Christ is to discern the Father of all, as manifested through his only-begotten Son incarnate… And thus the Gospels, which contain the memorials of this wonderful grace, are our principal treasures.  They may be called the text of the Revelation; and the Epistles, especially St Pauls’, are as comments upon it, unfolding and illustrating it in its various parts, raising history into doctrine, ordinances into sacraments, detached words or actions into principles, and this everywhere dutifully preaching his person, work and will.

 

Blessed John Henry Newman (1801-1890), Parochial and Plain Sermons, II, 154-55
