November is the month of the Holy Souls.  The names of those whom you wish to be remembered at Mass throughout the month can be placed in the casket before the altar; offerings can be placed in the box marked ‘Holy Souls’ by Our Lady’s altar.

 

 We need to recruit some more volunteers to help decorate the church throughout the year.  It is not an onerous task, but it is an important one; to provide flowers for the church and arrange them in the weeks of Ordinary Time.  You would be asked to do no more than half a dozen times during the course of a year.  If you would like to volunteer, please speak to Fr Sharp.

The archbishop has asked that the following insert be added:-

 

The Archdiocese hearing in the Independent Inquiry into Child Sexual Abuse (IICSA) begins in London on Monday 12 November and runs until Friday 16 November.

 

The Archdiocese believes that the work being done by IICSA is of great significance and supports the aim of strengthening and improving safeguarding practices and culture within the Church.

 

The welfare of children, young people and vulnerable adults is a fundamental priority for the Archdiocese.  Fostering a culture which has safeguarding at its very heart remains a key part of our work.

 

If you would like to speak to someone about safeguarding and the Church’s work, please call 0121 230 6240.

 

If you would like to speak to someone independent of the Church, you can contact the NSPCC on 0808 800 5000.

 

Please pray with us for all victims of abuse, and all those involved in the work of this important Inquiry.

 

Last weekend’s Pastoral Letter on this matter can be found on the archdiocesan website https://www.birminghamdiocese,org.uk [and there are copies at the back of church].
