November is the month of the Holy Souls.  The names of those whom you wish to be remembered at Mass throughout the month can be placed in the casket before the altar; offerings can be placed in the box marked ‘Holy Souls’ by Our Lady’s altar.

 

 We need to recruit some more volunteers to help decorate the church throughout the year.  It is not an onerous task, but it is an important one; to provide flowers for the church and arrange them in the weeks of Ordinary Time.  You would be asked to do no more than half a dozen times during the course of a year.  If you would like to volunteer, please speak to Fr Sharp.
