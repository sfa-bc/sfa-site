June is a month of devotion to the Most Sacred Heart of Jesus.  At the end of every Mass during the month, we shall recite the following prayer:

 

Sweet Jesus, through Your tender love for the Church, Your Spouse, You opened to her the riches of Your Sacred Heart; grant that our hearts may be enriched by the treasures it contains and replenished with its overflowing and unfailing delights.  Heart of Jesus, burning with love for us, inflame our hearts with love of You.

 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Further to the short item on the bulletin a couple of weeks ago about fundraising for the decoration of the church and restoration of the organ next year, a dedicated page has now been added to the parish website, and we are exploring the possibility of crowdfunding.

A simple idea that everyone could adopt would be to have a jar at home into which loose change can be placed; once full, the jar can be brought and emptied into the special donations box at the back of church.  This works!! Someone put a bag of coins in the box this past week with over £14 in it.

Please do think about doing something for which you can be sponsored or doing something like holding a coffee morning/wine party for your friends and neighbours; anything that will raise money.

Finally, and either in addition to the above or in its place, you might like to make a donation and pay for a specific area of the proposed decorative scheme.  At the back of church is a list of such items, ranging from £15 to £3,000.  You can make a single donation or pledge an amount and work to realise it over the next few months.

The Spirit dwells in the Church and in the hearts of the faithful as in a temple.  In them he prays and witnesses to the fact that they are adopted sons and daughters.  Guiding her into al truth and uniting her in communion and service, he bestows upon her various gifts, both hierarchic and charismatic, and adorns he with the fruits if his grace.

By the power of the gospel he allows the Church to keep the freshness of youth and leads her to perfect union with her spouse.  Thus, the Church shines forth as a people made one with the unity of the Father, Son, and the Holy Spirit.

The whole body of the faithful who have an anointing that comes from the holy one cannot err in matters of belief.  This characteristic is shown in the supernatural appreciation of the faith of the whole people of, when, from the bishops to the last one of the faithful, they manifest a universal consent in matters of faith and morals.  By this appreciation of the faith, aroused and sustained by the Spirit of truth, the People of God, guided by the sacred teaching authority and obeying it, receives not the mere word of men, but truly the word of God, the faith once delivered to the saints.

Constitution of the Second Vatican Council on the Church
