An Act of Consecration to the Sacred Heart of Jesus

 

O Sacred Heart of Jesus, filled with infinite love, broken by my ingratitude, pierced by my sin, yet loving me still, accept the consecration that I make to You of all that I have and all that I am.

Take every faculty of my soul and body and draw me day by day neared to Your Sacred Side, and there, as I may bear the lesson, teach me Your blessed ways. 

Teach me to be generous, teach me to serve You as You deserve, to give and not to count the cost, to fight and not to heed the wounds, to toil and not to seek for rest, to labour and not to seek reward, save that of knowing that I am doing Your will.

Hear of Jesus, give me only Your grace abd Your love, and then do with me what You will. Amen.
