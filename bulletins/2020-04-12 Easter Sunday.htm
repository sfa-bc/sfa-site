THE LORD HAS TRULY RISEN, ALLELUIA!

A HAPPY AND BLESSED EASTER TO EVERYONE

 

It is hard to have a sense of Easter joy in the midst of the current crisis, surrounded as we are by so much suffering and hardship, even as we are inspired and humbled by the many acts of kindness and of heroic endeavor on the part of so many who serve us.  We give thanks for all that they are doing, and we remember them in prayer, alongside those who are sick, those who mourn, and all who are anxious and are finding it very difficult to cope.  We pray also for the dead and the dying.

The message of Easter is that Christ has conquered death and that, whatever happens to us, we belong to him as he belongs to us.  Let us take comfort and solace in the knowledge that the love of God is greater than anything that befalls us.

 

Please keep in touch, either by telephone (above) or email (sfa.bc@outlook.com) and remember to pray for one another.

Rise heart; thy Lord is risen.  Sing his praise

                                        Without delayes,

Who takes thee by the hand, that thou likewise

                                        With him mayst arise:

That, as his death calcined thee to dust,

His life may make thee gold, and much more just.

 

Awake, my lute, and struggle for thy part

                                        With all thy art.

The crosse taught all wood to resound his name,

                                        Who bore the same.

His stretched sinews taught all strings, what key

Is best to celebrate this most high day.

 

George Herbert (1593-1633)
