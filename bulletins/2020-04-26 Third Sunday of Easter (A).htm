May is a month of special devotion to our Blessed Lady.  You might like to recite this prayer, the oldest known prayer to Mary, which will be said at the end of every Mass during the month:-

We fly to thy patronage, O holy Mother of God.  Despise not our prayers in our necessities but deliver us from all dangers, O glorious and blessed Virgin.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

O eternal Truth, what is your teaching, and what is the way by which you want us to go to the Father, the way by which we must go?  I know of no other road but the one paved with the true and solid virtues of your charity’s fire. You, eternal Word, cemented it with your blood, so this must be the road.

Our sin lies in nothing else but in loving what you hate and hating what you love.  I confess, eternal God, that I have constantly loved what you hate and hated what you love.  But today I cry out in the presence of your mercy: grant that I may follow your truth with a simple heart; give me the deep well and fire of charity; give me a continual hunger to endure pains and torments for you.  Eternal Father, give my eyes a fountain of tears with which to draw your mercy down over all the world and especially over your bride.

St Catherine of Siena (1347-1380)
