­-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Our first week back at Mass

There have been a few teething troubles, but we are getting into a new routine.  Numbers have been quite small – 10 last Sunday, 8 on Monday, 11 on Tuesday, 6 on Wednesday, 7 on Thursday – so at present there is no demand for extra Masses.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The renovation work in the church was originally planned to be finished this month, but delays caused by disruptions round the pandemic mean that it is likely to be late September or even October before we can move back into the church.  We are lucky to have the former Sisters’ Choir, even though it means that the numbers we can accommodate with the present restrictions reduces the number we could have in the church.

Our celebration on 4th October to mark the 150th anniversary of the opening of the church in 1870 will perforce be postponed.
