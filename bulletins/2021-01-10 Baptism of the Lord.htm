.

Earth bore me,

Water fed me,

Air bathed me,

Fire stirred me.

 

Earth my substance,

Water my fullness,

Air my strength,

Fire my joy.

 

I was lifted up,

I was cut down,

I was broken,

I was burned.

 

Earth I was,

Water bound me,

Air leavened me,

Fore formed me.

 

All four elements

Of the round worls

Meet in me

For man’s feeding.

 

For I am strength,

I am cleanness,

I am freedom,

I am light.

 

I am lifted up,

And draw men unto me,

The fair white body

Of God’s Son.

 

‘Bread’ by J.D.C. Pellow
