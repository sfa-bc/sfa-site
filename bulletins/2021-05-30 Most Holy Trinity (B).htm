May is traditionally a month of devotion to Our Lady.  At the end of every Mass we recite the following prayer, which dates from the third century and is the oldest known prayer to Our Lady:-

 

We fly to thy Patronage, O holy Mother of God.  Despise not our prayers in our necessities but deliver us from all dangers, O glorious and blessed Virgin.

 

June is traditionally a month of devotion to the Most Sacred Heart of Jesus.  This prayer can be used:-

 

Sweet Jesus, through Your tender love for the Church Your Spouse, You open to her the riches of Your Sacred Heart; grant that our hearts may be enriched by the treasures it contains and replenished with its overflowing and unfailing delights.

Heart of Jesus, burning with love for us, inflame our hearts with love of You.

A Message from Fr Sharp:-

 

This is my last weekend as parish priest of Baddesley Clinton after what has been, for me, twenty-one happy years.  I am sorry to be leaving and will miss the parish and parish life and, most of all, you, the parishioners.

I am grateful to Almighty God for allowing me the privilege of serving you all these years, and I thank you for the support you have given me and for the many acts of kindness and generosity you have shown me.

Looking back, I am conscious of having done some things well, but also of my inadequacies and many failures, the result, not of laziness, but of the defects and deficiencies of my character and personality.

I humbly ask your forgiveness for any hurt or upset I have caused you, and I entreat your prayers for me as I embark on the final stage of my earthly pilgrimage.

I am sure you will not have seen the last of me and hope that in the not too distant future, when all Covid restrictions have been lifted, I will be able to return to bid a proper farewell.
