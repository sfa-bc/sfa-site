PATRONAL FEAST OF ST FRANCIS OF ASSISI  9th/10th October

 

~  Patronal Feast of St Francis  In order to maximise attendance our Patronal Feast  has been transferred to the weekend of 9th/ 10th of October.

~ Family Fast Day Thank you for the return of your envelopes containing your offerings to CAFOD.  Returned envelopes can continue to be placed in the offertory bowl with the collection

~ Fr Hudsons  There are booklets giving details of the work of the Fr Hudson’s Society together with pre-paid envelopes for donations at the back of the church.

~ Weekday Masses - I have introduced an additional Mass each week on Wednesdays at 9.00am.  So there will be a Mass at 9.00am on Wednesdays and Thursdays at St Francis, Baddesley Clinton.  There will also be a Mass at Blessed Robert Grissold church, Balsall Common at 10.00am on Tuesdays (please note the Mass at BRG this week will be at 9.30am due to a Deanery Clergy meeting that Fr Frank must attend).                                                                                                                                               ~  October and the Rosary  October is the month of the Rosary and it is a good time therefore to renew our devotion to our Lord and our Blessed Lady through this rich

~ Masses at St Francis in Time of COVID-19 +  Attendees to wear a mask to protect themselves and others +  Remain 2 metres apart +  Sanitise hands on entering and exiting the Church +  Always follow the stewards’ instructions  + Holy Communion +  Masks to be worn to receive the Host + Please stay at home if... +  You are feeling ill or show any symptoms  +  You suspect you may be ill or have been exposed to Covid 19 +  You have a sick family member at home + You have come into contact with someone who is infected + Those attending will be asked to supply contact details for track and trace + Wrap up warm (if necessary) - doors and windows will be left open to increase ventilation wherever possible
