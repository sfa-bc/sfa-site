CHRISTMASTIDE 2021

 

Thanks to all those who have volunteered to ready the church for Christmas and for those assisting with the stewarding of the Masses.  Special thanks to Craig for enhancing our worship with music.  My gratitude, also, for the gifts, cards, generosity and support I have received.  May God bless you and your loved ones this Christmas.  Fr Frank.

 

Notice from the Archdiocese:  The wearing of face coverings (masks) is now mandatory in places of worship.  There are exemptions for priests, for the public proclamation of scriptural readings and for those who have a legitimate exemption

 

Christmas Offering  The collections at the Christmas Masses are for your priest.  Thank you for your gifts.

 

Pope Francis’ Invitation to the Synodal Process  In preparation for our participation in the Synodal Process I have devised a questionnaire to help us reflect, individually and in small groups, through prayerful reflection, discussion and listening, how we can respond to what the Holy Spirit is saying to us today.  I would be pleased to receive written responses to those questions which most interest you and I will use your written responses in formulating a response to the Archdiocese who will forward a summary of the feedback from parishes to the Synod on Synodality in Rome in 2023.  The questionnaires are available at the back of the church.

Masses at St Francis in Time of COVID-19  +  Attendees to wear a mask to protect themselves and others +  Remain 2 metres apart +  Sanitise hands on entering and exiting the Church +  Always follow the stewards’ instructions  + Holy Communion +  Masks to be worn to receive the Host + Please stay at home if... +  You are feeling ill or show any symptoms  +  You suspect you may be ill or have been exposed to Covid 19 +  You have a sick family member at home + You have come into contact with someone who is infected + Those attending will be asked to supply contact details for track and trace + Wrap up warm (if necessary) - doors and windows will be left open to increase ventilation wherever possible
