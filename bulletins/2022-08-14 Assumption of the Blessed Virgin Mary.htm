Solemnity of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary

 

The saints are our models of Christian life.  This is especially true of Mary because of the place she occupies in the history of salvation.  Mary is the perfect Christian and as such she is the immaculate model for the whole Church.  She can be seen as the Church in miniature.  She shows us that we must be dedicated completely to Christ as she was, that we must be willing to suffer with him, as she did.  And she also shows us that loyalty to Christ guarantees immortality, body as well as soul in complete happiness in heaven.
