FIFTH SUNDAY IN ORDINARY TIME

 

Priest:  Fr Frank Smith (07512 100 908)  Email:  fr.frank.smith@rcaob.org.uk

Parish Website:  www.sfachurch.org.uk 

 

In the Gospel today Jesus teaches that the lives of his followers should have a distinctive quality.  Like salt, we should add a peculiar flavour to life because our life in Christ is distinctive and peculiar - different from what the world has to offer.  This distinctive and peculiar quality of the Christ-life in us should shine like a light in the world, because Christ is the Light of the world.  But if we lose our distinctive and peculiar taste - if the light in us is dimmed or hidden through sin, or compomised with the world - then we are no longer fit for purpose and we fail in our witness to the love and light of Christ - a witness that is vital in order that all may “taste and see that the Lord is good.”
