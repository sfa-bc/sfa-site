THIRTY-FIRST SUNDAY IN ORDINARY TIME

 

November is the month the Church especially remembers to pray for the dead.  Remembrance Sunday is next week when we will pray for the repose of the souls of all those who have lost their lives as a result of war and we continue to pray for our deceased loved ones throughout the month of November.  Scriptures and the Church teach that we can help the departed through the offering of the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass for them. Consequently, Masses will be offered for the Holy Souls over the course of November.  

 

Second collection this Sunday There will be a second collection this Sunday for the Johnson Association.

Holy Souls – There will be Masses offered for the Holy Souls throughout the month of November.  The names of those who have been presented will be named in the Intercessions.

Parish Council As agreed at our previous meeting, we will have a Parish Council Meeting in November/December.  Watch the bulletin for further details.

Deanery website For details of Masses and events in the Deanery please see: https://warwick-catholicdeanery.org.uk/

The opening Service of the Visitation is on Tuesday 7th November, 7pm at St Peter’s, Leamington Spa. Vespers and Benediction with Archbishop Bernard. Bicester parish will host closing Vespers.

Remembrance Sunday – Next Sunday is Remembrance Sunday when we will remember and pray for the war dead of our Parish.
