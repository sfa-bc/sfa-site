FOURTH SUNDAY OF LENT

Today is Mothering Sunday and so we give thanks to God for our mothers and for all who are mothers in our community.  May God bless you in your high calling, remembering that the greatest of the saints, Our Blessed Lady, was, like you, a mother and that through loving devotion to her calling attained to the heights of holiness.

Additional devotions in Lent:

  • Tuesdays Stations of the Cross will be prayed after the Masses on Tuesdays during Lent at Blessed Robert Grissold church, Balsall Common.
  • Wednesdays The Holy Rosary will be prayed after the 9.00am Mass on Wednesdays at St Francis church, Baddesley Clinton.  The Rosary will be followed by a Lent Course.
  • Thursdays Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament will follow the 9.00am Mass on Thursdays at St Francis church, Baddesley Clinton.  Adoration will finish at 10.00am with Benediction.
  • Fridays Stations of the Cross will follow the 9.00am Mass on Fridays at St Francis church, Baddesley Clinton.
