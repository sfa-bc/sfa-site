TENTH SUNDAY IN ORDINARY TIME

The Gospel of Christ is something so radically good and new that it can be difficult to accept - even by those who are closest to Jesus.  The Gospel today tells us that Jesus’ own relatives thought he was “out of his mind.”  And others accuse him of siding with Satan and with those who oppose God.  They do not have “eyes to see” or “ears to hear” and so his message seems like foolishness or worse - like blasphemy against God - Such is their blindness and deafness to the true God who lives and speaks in Christ.

June - the Month of the Sacred Heart  June is the month of the Sacred Heart.  Great benefits are promised to those who practice this devotion.  Also, during this month, we can seek to deepen our devotion to the love of God revealed in the Most Sacred Heart of Jesus - a devotion encouraged by St Margaret Mary, whose relic rests in the altar at Blessed Robert Grissold church.  We might even consider consecrating ourselves to the Sacred Heart of our Lord Jesus Christ. 

Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament at BRG will follow the 7.00pm Mass on Tuesdays every first Tuesday of the month. Exposition of the Blessed Sacrament will be followed by Benediction at 8.00pm.  All welcome.

 

Study of John’s Gospel Fr Frank is leading a study of John’s Gospel at Saint Francis after the 9.00am Mass and the Holy Rosary each Wednesday.  All welcome.

Please see cafod.org.uk/encounter or svp.org.uk/encounter for more information.
