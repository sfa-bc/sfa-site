EIGHTEENTH SUNDAY IN ORDINARY TIME

The journey to the God of life is not an easy task.  We tend to get lost along the way and we are discouraged by the efforts we have to make.  On their way to the promised land, the Jewish people turned against Moses - their difficulties made them yearn for a mediocre but familiar existence.  Freedom frightened them, slavery being more familiar seemed more secure.  This is the temptation of every believer and this is why Jesus says to his disciples:  “Do not be afraid.”

Scam Alert We believe that parishioners who have travelled with an independent travel company, on tours to religious sites are being targeted.  Fraudsters pretending to be from the travel company are writing to parishioners who have travelled on a religious tour asking for donations. They are saying they have fallen on hard times and provide their bank account details so people can make a donation.  Please do not make a donation – this is a  scam. If you have made a donation, please report it to the police at Action Fraud on telephone number 0300 123 2040 or online at actionfraud.police.uk and report it to your bank. (Dial 159 to be automatically connected to your bank’s fraud prevention service).
