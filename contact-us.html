St. Francis of Assisi Catholic Church

Baddesley Clinton is a small village in Warwickshire, 7 miles from Warwick and 12 miles from Stratford-upon-Avon. It is 15 miles from the centre of the City of Birmingham and is near to both the M40 and M42 motorways.

The Catholic Church of St Francis of Assisi serves a parish of approximately 35 square miles (58 square kilometers) and comprises the villages of Baddesley Clinton, Chadwick End, Lapworth, Rowington, Wroxall, Honiley, Beausale, and Haseley.
