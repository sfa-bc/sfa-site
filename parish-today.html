St. Francis of Assisi Catholic Church

Today the parish continues in much the same way as it has for the last century or so, an oasis of rural tranquility in a busy, industrial and commercial world. The number of parishioners is small, a mixture of the well-established, whose families have lived here for some time, and more recent arrivals, and there is a constant and steady stream of visitors.

We are determined to maintain this historic Mission and to continue to preserve the Faith for which our ancestors were persecuted and some died. Please help us by your prayers and be assured of ours for you.

Aided by the prayers of Our Lady, St Francis and St Clare, may the Lord be with you at all times and, wherever you are, may you be with Him throughout all time.
